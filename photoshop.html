Instruction for cropping

1.Using this cropper ,you can crop your image/video even into zig-zag shape

2.To use this cropping feature,move your mouse to the image/video and start drawing the map of dots over your image/video of whatever area you want to crop-out by clicking.These dots are meant for the boundary of the part of image/video that will be finally cropped out ,so be careful in putting dots.

3.on each clicking ,a small dot will appear at the clicked position,continue on drawing your map

onclicking the "Done" button,the part of the image/video entrapped between the dots will be cropped off.

4.After finishing your map,click the button "Done".
If you think you have commited some mistake in previous step,click reset button,this will clear-out one dot.
The minimum number of dots is always 3

5.You can readjust your cropped-image(or video) even after finishing.
To do this ,you need to click cropping button again
Press the crop button in full screen mode only,otherwise image/video may not be loaded completely.
Rest is alright😉️😉️



🙂️That's all for using this feature!!🙂️
